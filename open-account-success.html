Leveraged margin trading :
By using a small amount of funds to make leveraged investments. This means you are depositing a fraction of the full value of your trade. Leveraged trading can significantly increase the rate of return, or it can multiply the loss several times and you may lose more than the deposited funds, so leveraged trading may not be suitable for all investors.
